About

Starting in childhood, solving most types of logic problems is a favorite pasttime. This included figuring out how to write a basic computer program to print my name or calculate my age on my families first computers. In a small town, there were few opportunities to go any further in computer science and the influence of my family's pharmacy led me to pursue chemistry. After obtaining a PhD in chemistry and conducting research to improve detection and treatment of cancer my interest in computers was reignited. I am currently looking to work as a software developer.
